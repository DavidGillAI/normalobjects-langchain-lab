The LangChain agent handled complaints creatively by selecting available tools and combining their perspectives into final responses. In testing, it often used both the Demogorgon tool and the NDGT science-perspective tool, showing flexible tool chaining rather than a fixed workflow. This made the answers playful and adaptable, but it also meant the agent sometimes expanded beyond the limited information returned by the tools. Compared with a structured LangGraph approach, this LangChain agent is better for open-ended creative tasks, while LangGraph would be better when a predictable order of steps is required.
